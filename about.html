About e-Khedut

A Gujarat Government initiative to bridge the gap between farmers and markets through technology, transparency, and trust.

Our Mission

e-Khedut was founded by Agriculture, Farmers Welfare and Co-operation Department of the Government of Gujarat to empower Indian farmers with digital tools that help them get fair prices for their produce, access real-time information, and connect directly with buyers across the country.

State believe that technology can bridge the gap between rural farms and urban markets, eliminating middlemen and ensuring that farmers receive the true value of their hard work.

e-Khedut platform provides not just a marketplace, but a complete ecosystem — weather updates, water and electricity schedules, crop demand insights, and live market prices.
